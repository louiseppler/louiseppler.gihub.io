Contapp QR

Download for iOS Download for Android

App currently not available for download

When you meet someone and want to exchange phone numbers, dictating numbers is cumbersome and slow. Some use business cards, but the details are not automatically saved to your contacts.

Contapp makes it possible to use QR codes to share contact details. It's easy and usable by anyone. It doesn't require a subscription, you can share your contacts directly from your phone, even when you're both offline. And the recipient doesn't even need the app!
